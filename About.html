About Us – Abeda-e-Dastarkhwan

Welcome to Abeda-e-Dastarkhwan, where every dish tells a story of heritage, flavor, and love. Established in 2024, our restaurant was born from a deep passion for authentic Mughlai and traditional Indian cuisine — passed down through generations.

At Abeda-e-Dastarkhwan, we believe food is not just to fill your stomach, but to touch your soul. Our menu is a carefully curated journey of rich biryanis, creamy curries, mouth-watering kebabs, and delightful desserts — all cooked with handpicked spices and a lot of heart.

Whether you're here for a family dinner, a celebration, or a peaceful solo meal, our royal yet cozy ambience will make you feel at home. With every bite we aim to bring you the warmth of home-cooked meals and the luxury of a royal dastarkhwan.

Our mission is simple — to serve unforgettable taste, unmatched quality, and heartfelt hospitality.

Come, be a part of our story. Let your tastebuds travel through tradition.

Meet Our Master Chef

His signature dishes include the royal dum biryani and melt-in-the-mouth shahi tukda.
